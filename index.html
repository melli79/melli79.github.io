Hello, I am Melchior Grutzmann a software programmer at zollSoft GmbH in Jena, Germany. 2012 till 2013 I was a lecturer of the Department of Mathematics at Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China. 2010–2012 I did a post-doc at Sun Yat-sen university in mathematics. In 2005-2009 I studied mathematics as a Ph.D. student under Ping Xu. In 1999-2004 I studied physics at the Friedrich Schiller University in Jena, Germany at the Department of Physics and Astronomy.

I was teaching Calculus and Abstract algebra in Fall.

In my spare time I'm learning languages such as Chinese or Japanese, read books, or do cycling.

My research area is non-Riemannian differential geometry such as Lie and Courant algebroids, Poisson and symplectic geometry, Lie groupoids and gerbes. My publications are online at arXiv.org.
